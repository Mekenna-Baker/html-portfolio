My Favorite Movies!

Mekenna Baker

  1. The Labyrinth (1986)

    2. This movie is a classic! While incredibly cheesy, I love David Bowie, and I think this was such a unique idea for a film!

  2. Juno (2007)

    3. I love this movie so much. Juno (Elliot Page) is so quippy, and holds her head high while navigating a teen pregnancy. Despite others opinions, she stands her ground and does a very wonderful thing.

  3. Crazy, Stupid, Love (2011)

    4. The best feel good movie. While facing a divorce Cal, (Steve Carell) rediscovers himself, and is trying to reconnect with his wife (Julianne Moore) and his three children.
